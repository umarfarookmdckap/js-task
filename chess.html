Chess Board

(click a piece then a target square to move — simple demo)

About this demo

This is a single-file HTML/CSS/JS chessboard. It uses a grid to render 8×8 squares and places pieces using Unicode chess glyphs. Click a square with a piece to select it, then click a destination square to move. It does not enforce chess rules — moves are free. Use Reset and Flip Board to change the view.

If you want: legal move generation, drag & drop, or a basic AI opponent, tell me and I'll add it.
